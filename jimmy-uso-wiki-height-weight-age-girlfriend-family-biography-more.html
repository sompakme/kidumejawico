    Jimmy Uso is a professional wrestler who is associated with WWE. He along with his identical twin brother Jey created the tag team ‘The Usos’. Their team participates at professional wrestling championships across the world.

    He was born on 22nd August 1985 in San Francisco. He is carrying forward the name of Anoa’i family. Wrestling has become an ancestral profession of his family. All the famous wrestlers like The Rock, Yokozuna, Roman Reigns and Umaga belonged to the American Samoa wrestling family.

He has a twin brother Jey and three other siblings Joseph Fatu, Jeremiah Peniata Fatu, and Thavana Monalisa Fatu. Since childhood, he started learning wrestling moves from his father.

    Jimmy wanted to become a professional football player. He grew up in Florida and attended the Escambia High School. He played football during his school days. He was enrolled at the University of West Alabama on a partial scholarship.

    Though he wanted to become a professional footballer the idea failed. So, along with his brother, he started working at a furniture store. Simultaneously, they also participated in wrestling events. By participating in amateur wrestling events, he became a professional wrestler.

    In 2007, he made his professional wrestling debut at Florida based independent wrestling promotion “WXW”. In April 2010, they fought for the “FCW Tag Team Championship” and defeated Hunico and Tito Nieves.

    In 2010, the team of a brother made their debut at WWE. Later they started participating in “WWE Smackdown” in April 2011. In the event “WWE Superstar”, he had few solo fights in 2012. In 2014, he was ranked 25, among Pro Wrestling Illustrated. He has won both WWE tag-team championship and WWE Smackdown tag-team championship twice.

    He married his long-time girlfriend Trinity on January 16, 2014, in Hawaii.
