    T.I. Dusts Off His Freestyle Skills For Power 106 LA

    08 Aug 2024, 00:00

    T.I. delivers a brand new freestyle on his visit to the West Coast. Guesting on Power 106 LA with host Justin Credible, the Atlanta native delivered a compelling freestyle over Dr. Dre’s “F**k Wit Dre Day” and “Lil Ghetto Boy” before taking on Nipsey Hussle’s “Victory Lap”

    “Old dime a dozen a** ni**a you are not rare,” raps the 43-year-old. “21 years in this motherfu**er, top tier/ We are not peers, when we cheer it is not beer.”

    He continued flexing his rhyme skills and paying respect to the likes of 21 Savage, Snoop Dogg, Warren G, and Dr. Dre. The freestyle also addressed allegations and legal paperwork, seemingly referencing claims of sexual assault made against him and his wife, R&B singer Tiny.

    The freestyle comes as the “Bring Em Out” performer gears up to release what he says to be his final album.

    “I’m working my way up to it,” explained the rapper in 2023. “I been making some music, and the music I’m making is… how can I say? It’s expanding beyond just the concept that I have for my last album.”

    He continued to detail, “So my last album is Kill the King, which will be, you know, it’s gon’ be edgy, energetic… It’s gon’ have a lot of Tip in it… but I’ve been making somewhat, I guess, more melodic, you know, more R&B vibes, Afrobeat-R&B vibe, party music.”

    The album is preceded by the track “LLOGCLAY” featuring YoungBoy Never Broke Again, released earlier this year. Give it a listen below.
