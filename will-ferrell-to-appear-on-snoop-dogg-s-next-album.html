    Will Ferrell To Appear On Snoop Doggs Next Album?

    Funny man Will Ferrell may just be tapping into his inner rap star once again, this time along side, Snoop D-O-double G. During his Ask Me Anything Reddit session, the Anchorman star got a special request from the Doggfather to help him out on his next album.

    21 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Funny man Will Ferrell may just be tapping into his inner rap star once again, this time along side, Snoop D-O-double G. During his ‘Ask Me Anything’ Reddit session, the Anchorman star got a special request from the Doggfather to help him out on his next album.

    During Ferrell’s Reddit “Ask Me Anything” session, Snoop interrupted with a hefty proposition, “Ayy Will, ima need some more cowbell on tha next album, u game?”

    The man also known as Ron Burgundy responded, “Of course! I’m absolutely game, Snoop. I’ve also got some beats that I want to run by you. I’m warning you now, they’re not good. Edit: In fact, I just talked myself out of it. I’m not going to show them to you.”

    The two go on to say that a “Boats N’ Hoes” sequel, a nod to the laugh track from the 2008 Step Brothers, may be in order, but not without climbing some trees first.

    Will need this collaboration STAT.
