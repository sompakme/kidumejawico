    Many people are familiar with conservative activist and author Candace Owens (and her notorious Twitter feuds), but what does the public know about her husband, George Farmer? The Blackout author and her husband married at the Trump Winery in Charlottesville, Virginia back in 2019 after a whirlwind romance; the couple had only met several months before, and George popped the question just three weeks after first setting eyes on each other.

    Their relationship moved so quickly that Candace herself has joked that the relationship “sounds made up." Love can be stranger than fiction, however, and the happy couple wound up welcoming two children and seem as happy as ever today.

    So what is there to know about Candace Owens' husband?

    UPDATE: 2023/05/17 08:05 EST BY LANE VASQUEZ

    George Farmer Seems To Be Enjoying Fatherhood & Married Life With Candace Owens

    George Farmer and Candace Owens may have had a whirlwind romance, but they now have two children together, and George is said to enjoy being a dad.

    10 George Is The Son Of Lord Farmer

    George is from the UK, and he's just as involved in politics as his wife is. He used to be the chairman of Turning Point UK, but he's also the son of a Conservative party member.

    His father was the treasurer and a member of the House of the Lords.

    Technically, George is the Honorable George, as his title seems to have been handed down from his father.

    9 George Farmer Was Educated At Oxford

    George is highly educated and has attended elite institutions in the UK. He is a graduate of the prestigious Oxford University, and performed well during his studies, as he left with a First Class degree in Theology.

    While studying at Oxford, Farmer was reportedly also a member of the highly secretive Bullingdon Club, which is famous for holding riotous parties around the city.

    Prior to this, George attended St Paul's School, London, a private school that was founded back in 1503 and has educated a number of important figures in British society.

    8 George Has Strong Political Opinions

    Just like his wife, George has built up a career in the political field. Turning Point UK, his former employer, encourages right-wing politics and thinking in British schools and higher education institutions.

    Related: Cardi B Ferociously Attacks Candace Owens And Donald Trump In A Must-See Video

    George used to be a member of the UK Conservative Party. In 2016, he became the youngest-ever member of the Leader’s Group and has also chaired the Black and White Ball for the Conservative party.

    However, he changed his political allegiance to the Brexit Party, due to his anti-EU feelings, having described the EU as “a toxic, socialist, genocidal superstate."

    Farmer was previously the CEO of Parler, a conservative social network, too, though he's said to have sold the network to Ye West.

    7 George And Candace Met At A UK Event

    Farmer met his future wife back in December 2018 at a soft launch reception party for Turning Point UK. The attraction was immediate, according to Owens, and the two immediately began their romance, bonding quickly over their shared political views and sense of humor.

    They apparently had a long-distance relationship for a time, before getting married very quickly.

    6 He And Candace Enjoyed A Whirlwind Romance

    Just three weeks after they met, George popped the question.

    After a brief engagement, after Farmer proposed over FaceTime, ostensibly from a distance, the couple married on August 31st, 2019, in a stunning ceremony at the Trump Winery in Charlottesville, Virginia.

    The wedding was a highly-profiled event.

    Writing on Instagram in 2019, celebrating Thanksgiving as a newlywed, Candace called her husband 'the greatest gift' that year, writing, "my husband— the greatest gift of this year. Your love has humbled me in ways I never knew was possible. I could never give enough thanks to the miracle of you."

    5 Candace And George's Wedding Was MAGA-Themed

    Given their conservative leanings, it probably made sense to attendees that Candace Owens' husband was on board with her wedding theme.

    The couple's nuptials were said to include a "MAGA cringe dance party" and a cake that featured a "Make Weddings Great Again" hat. In wedding photos, the two can be seen wearing hats that reflect their political beliefs; Candace wore a red MAGA hat, along with her bridesmaids and guests.

    4 Candace And George Had A Son In 2021

    Candace announced her pregnancy about midway through, and she and George welcomed their first child, a son, in 2021. The little boy's name doesn't seem to have been made public.

    3 The Two Welcomed A Daughter In 2022

    In 2022, Candace and George welcomed their second child, a daughter, whose name is Louise Marie Farmer. George is apparently a hands-on father and is enjoying parenthood with his wife.

    Candace has praised her family and her husband for supporting her.

    I have never felt more certain of who I am, more sure of where my place is in this world, or more steady on my own two feet.

    People ask me all the time where I get my confidence from. The better question is where I get my happiness from:

    At home—exactly where my heart is.

    As a mother, I will discuss anything and everything—from vaccines to overseas conflicts— and I will not blink twice at those who try to intimidate me away from digging deeper.

    I have learned there is narrative and there is truth and that the two are more often than not, not the same.

    When it comes to my household, me and my husband are the experts.

    2 George Is Very Supportive Of His Wife

    Though George is less interested in a public role, despite his time in the spotlight, he is tremendously supportive of his wife and her career. Candace frequently posts loving photos of her and her husband on her social media pages.

    Candace Owens' husband also made an appearance on The Candace Owens Show, including right before their wedding weekend.

    George reportedly fell head over heels when he first met his future wife, to the extent that he felt 'nothing else mattered.' He is also said to have described the now-Mrs. Farmer as "the most beautiful woman in the world."

    1 Candace Considers Her Husband A "Manly" Man

    Candace gushes praise for her husband on social media all the time, but she's also dragged men she deems less "manly."

    Harry Styles notably shaded Candace by quoting her "Bring back manly men" quip on social media. Her husband doesn't seem to have any concerns, though.

    He clearly supports his wife's viewpoints, and the two seem to be a perfect match.
