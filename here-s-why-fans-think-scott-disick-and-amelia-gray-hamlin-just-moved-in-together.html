    Amelia is currently 19 and now taking a major life step. Is she taking that step with Scott? Here's what we know.

    Amelia Just Moved House

    This post to Amelia's IG this weekend gave fans just enough information to get their heads spinning about where the model lives. She clearly moved, but where to?

    Most fans seem to think "Scott's casa" is the answer. Scott has been known to move girlfriends into his homes when things get serious. His ex Sofia Richie moved into his Malibu place back in 2019, telling viewers of 'Flip It Like Disick' (an E! show about Scott's properties) that "it was like a bachelor pad" before she lived there.

    Since Amelia and Scott have been getting closer and closer, fans in Amelia's IG comments section are convinced that this move was designed to literally bring the model closer to him.

    Some went so far as calling Amelia's move "weird since Sophia lived there," and claiming that she's probably joining a "dorm with Sophia."

    More creative fans kept it general with questions like "moving to Malibu?" and  "Where should I send your moving gift?"

    Lisa Rinna Commented

    So Amelia's 'Real Housewives' cast member mama comments on basically everything she posts, but this time fans of the family think her words might contain clues to the couple's current whereabouts.

    "Shes right back where she belongs," Lisa wrote on Amelia's moving post.

    The top response to that comment is "did she move in with Scott Disick?" but unfortunately Lisa has yet to respond.

    Other fans speculate that 'where she belongs' might mean Amelia is somewhere close to the Hamlin family whether or not she's bunking with Scott (who FYI has bleached eyebrows right now to go with his new look).

    "She could be meaning LA or Bev Hills in general," one commenter guessed, with others adding "it can be perceived either way but whatever, Mum seems happy!"
