    Chris Harrison’s Defends Rachael Kirkconnell Over Racist Photo

    Rachael Kirkconnell, a contestant, and winner on Matt James’ season of The Bachelor. While the show was being aired it was unknown that Rachael was the inevitable winner. So, when her racist pictures leaked of Rachael at a college party and of her liking, insensitive social media posts, fans, and members of Bachelor Nation, including Matt James were not happy.
