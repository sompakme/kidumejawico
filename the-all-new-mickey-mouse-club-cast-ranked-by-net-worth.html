    Disney has a reputation for building young stars. Today, we see people like Hilary Duff and Selena Gomez as stars who began on Disney. It has become a platform for launching the career of very young talent. The kind of young people working at Disney are certainly among the most talented in their chosen field.

    In the early to mid-90s, The All-New Mickey Mouse Club was the show to watch on DIsney Channel. The show featured some legendary cast members including Britney Spears, Ryan Gosling, Justin Timberlake, and Christina Aguilera. The show let the world see their talent, but very few could've predicted how successful their careers would become.

    But which cast member is the richest? Let's find out.

    8 Nikki DeLoach - $1 Million

    Nikki DeLoach had quite the interesting journey which began with her role on The All-New Mickey Mouse Club. DeLoach was pursuing a career in music after the show ended. DeLoach landed a spot in the girl group Innosense. This is the same group Britney Spears almost joined before her manager decided to shop Spears around as a solo artist. Lynn Harless, Justin Timberlake's mother, managed Innosense. Following the breakup of the group, DeLoach focused completely on acting.

    7 Keri Russell - $12 Million

    While Keri Russell's net worth is listed as $12 million, her salary is estimated to be $100 thousand per episode. Russell was one of many cast members of The All-New Mickey Mouse Club who made a name for herself starting in the late '90s. Russell is best known for her role in the WB series Felicity. Russell also appeared in The Americans and was nominated for an Emmy for her work. The role also earned Russell several Golden Globe Awards. Russell appeared in Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker. Russell was given a star on The Hollywood Walk of Fame in 2017.

    6 Tony Lucca - $16 Million

    Tony Lucca was one of several talented kids who made it onto The All-New Mickey Mouse Club. He was born in Pontiac, Michigan. His family was very musical and there were a lot of talented relatives that Lucca grew up with. By the time he was 12, Lucca was playing in bands in the Detroit area with his cousin Cole. Lucca auditioned for The All-New Mickey Mouse Club and when he got the job, his sister encouraged him to join the cast. After the show ended, Lucca appeared in some commercials, including ones for Blockbuster Video, Levi Jeans and J.C Penny. Lucca eventually began his own independent music career with production help from fellow Mouseketeer JC Chasez.

    5 JC Chasez - $16 Million

    JC Chasez was another future star on The All-New Mickey Mouse Club. He went on to join the group NSYNC and rose to stardom from there. NSYNC were among the biggest boy bands of the time. After the group disbanded, Chasez tried his hand at some solo musical projects. He also became a judge on the reality competition show America's Best Dance Crew. Chasez also writes and produces for other artists. Chasez has lent his talents to artists such as Girls Aloud, Sugababes, Basement Jaxx, David Archuleta, and Matthew Morrison.

    4 Ryan Gosling - $70 Million

    Ryan Gosling started as a dancer when he was a kid. His audition for The All-New Mickey Mouse Club would prove to be successful. When he joined the show, Gosling added several other skills to his list of talents. He went on to become a very famous actor with the 2004 romantic drama The Notebook. Gosling would go on to do films like Crazy, Stupid, Love and La La Land. His versatility as an actor has made him one of the most respected figures in his field today.

    Related: How Ryan Gosling Really Felt About His Time On The All-New Mickey Mouse Club

    3 Britney Spears - $70 Million

    The Princess of Pop was just a pre-teen when she was on The All-New Mickey Mouse Club. Spears was a born performer from the very beginning. Her music career started with a bang when her first single, "...Baby One More Time," was released. Its video unleashed Spears into the new wave of teen pop. Spears would grow beyond that phase, however, and rank up an impressive string of hits. She starred in the 2002 film Crossroads, proving her acting talent is still alive and well. She's now known as one of the most iconic pop stars of her generation.

    2 Christina Aguilera - $160 Million

    With a big voice and stage presence, Christina Aguilera was one of the stand-outs on The All-New Mickey Mouse Club. Her vocal ability made her a natural star and nobody was surprised when she began her singing career. Through the years, Aguilera achieved a ton of hit singles from "Genie In a Bottle," "Beautiful," and "Ain't No Other Man." Her career even extended into the acting realm when she starred alongside Cher in the film Burlesque. Aguilera has become a pop icon of her time.

    Related: Everything Britney Spears' Mickey Mouse Club Castmates Have Said About Her

    1 Justin Timberlake - $250 Million

    Justin Timberlake was a memorable part of The All-New Mickey Mouse Club. His performances on the show, especially the ones with Spears, would live on years after the show ended. He rose to fame when he was a member of the boy band NSYNC. They reached extremely high levels of success and earned a ton of hit singles. Timberlake eventually went solo and made a name for himself in his own right. Timberlake even stepped into the acting realm.
