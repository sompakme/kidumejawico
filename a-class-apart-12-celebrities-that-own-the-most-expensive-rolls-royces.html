    Since 1904 Rolls-Royce has been making the ultimate luxury vehicle for celebrities and the upper class. While stars in a variety of industry's own one, from NBA players to Hollywood's elite, some have requested special amenities and design from the top vehicle manufacturer, often causing the price tag to skyrocket.

    Though some like Queen Latifah, Simon Cowell, and Gwen Stefani are seen driving their luxurious cars, other celebrities such a Jay Leno, Justin Bieber, and Ludacris want to add a special touch to their massive collections.

    Here's some of the most expensive Rolls Royce's to date and the millionaires that own them.

    Jay-Z and Beyonce

    The power couple has more money than they know what to do with and are known to own some of the most exotic cars in the world. They enjoy vintage classics as well as recent collectibles like the Rolls-Royce Phantom VIII and a Silver Cloud III. Those two vehicles alone cost the superstars over $1 million.

    Kylie Jenner

    Each member of the Kardashian clan seems to have some classic and luxurious cars at their disposal, but what makes Jenner's Rolls-Royce Ghost special is the custom scarlet leather interior and starlight roof. According to The Things, with the additional add-ons, her purchase ran the mogul around $450,000.

    Shaquille O’Neal

    The former basketball legend has not only been known to have a massive car collection, but he's not opposed to gifting the classics to others in the past. Shaq owns a 17-car garage, which houses a Rolls-Royce Phantom, customized for his massive build. In 2020, the former NBA star had his Phantom customized to include an image of him and late Laker's legend Kobe Bryant on the car's hood.

    RELATED: The Most Coveted Cars From Ron Pratte's Collection

    Simon Cowell

    The 'American Idol' alum and 'America's Got Talent' judge is worth an estimated $550 million. He's also a car collector and enjoys driving around town in his Silver Cloud III. This particular model is one of the best in the vintage collection, as just 2,000 units were produced. In addition, Cowell boasts two Rolls-Royce Phantoms in his collection, among many others. One of the Phantoms alone is estimated to be worth more than $500,000.

    Floyd Mayweather Jr.

    The superstar boxer likes to win both in and out of the ring. He's been known to pay cash for his luxury vehicles, and his current collection is worth upwards of $1 million – almost all of which is Rolls-Royce. According to Did You Know Cars, Mayweather's Phantom VIII is rumored to cost more than $400,000.

    Queen Latifah

    She used to drive around in a Mercedes-Benz (stolen in 2016), but her new classic car is a $370,000 Rolls-Royce Phantom. It's worth triple what the Benz cost her, but it appears she's enjoying the custom features and classy ride.

    Jay Leno

    The former late-night host and comedian have shown off his classic car collection numerous times over the years. Hot Cars estimates his massive showroom to be worth more than $50 million, among them two matching 1937 Rolls-Royce Chassis.

    Jennifer Lopez

    The singer, actress, and the superstar has been seen traveling in her black Rolls-Royce Ghost for ultimate comfort and amenities. The estimated worth is around $320,000, but according to Money Inc., customizations can drain the bank of nearly $600,000.

    Gwen Stefani

    She's the frontwoman to rock band 'No Doubt', a newlywed to country music star Blake Shelton and an avid Rolls-Royce fan. Stefani routinely travels around town in her Wraith, worth somewhere between $300,000 and $600,000 based on customized requests. Stefani has a bright blue version and totes her three children to and from events in the classic car.

    Jamie Foxx

    Actor and singer Jamie Foxx owns a Rolls-Royce Phantom Drophead Coupe, worth $500,000. He must be attached to the collectable car; back in 2015, Foxx dropped around $20,000 for a new paint job and additional upgrades. The color changed from black to a satin white finish, tan interior and 24-inch Giovanna Rims.

    David Beckham

    The former soccer superstar has been seen driving his kids around in his $407,000 Phantom Drophead Coupe. Called the 'Holy Grail' of luxury automobiles, it's not surprising that the Rolls is at the top of his list in a large car collection. With convertible top and rearward opening coach doors, no wonder this model sets itself apart from others.

    50 Cent

    With several Rolls-Royce's in his collection already, 50 Cent went all out with the Cullinan, Rolls' first SUV with all-wheel drive. With a top speed of 155 mph, the famous rapper bought a Matte Orange model for his 44th birthday. The cost for the Cullinan runs around $330,000.
