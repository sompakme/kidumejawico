    Sister is a movie that explains the relationship of three sisters Diamond, Tia, and Tara with their grandmother.

    The movie is directed, written, and executive produced by Jahmar Hill.

    The 34-year-old Jahmar Hill is a writer, director, creator, and actor. Originally from Elizabeth, New Jersey, he now resides in Los Angeles, California where Sisters was also filmed.

    Hill previously worked for Warner Brothers and has worked in many TV shows such as Netflix’s Lucifer, Lethal Weapon from Fox, and many more.

    This is Hill’s fourth feature film written and directed by him.

    Many other Hill’s films such as Fourth Quarter, Run, and Last Love Lost are available to watch on TUBI as well as Amazon Prime.

    The crime, drama thriller movie was released with a 1 hour and 40 minutes run-time on August 3, 2023, on BET+ which will air on September on BET.

    Ciera Angelia plays the role of Diamond. She is one of the lead characters.

    Sister Bet Plus Movie Review

    Sister Bet Plus Movie Review is positive. It gives an insight into the plot of the story and the characters along with the behind-the-movie story.

    The story expresses the level of extent someone can go for the sake of protecting their loved ones.

    After the early demise of their parents, the girls were taken care of by their grandmother (played by Cocco Brown), who has a great impact on their lives.

    The movie depicts a friendly, and funny bond between the family of four.

    When the family finds out that Pat is suffering from Cancer, they are shattered and numb for a while.

    Money is the main factor that delays the treatment. Therefore, to save her grandmother, the sisters resort to illegal ways.

    Overall, the movie has comedy, thriller, action, and emotion. These many elements have proven the film to be entertaining and successful.

    In an interview with Reel Urban News, the director of the movie opened up about the behind stories of Sisters and his journey in the film industry.

    The script was ready by 2018 and shooting began in 2019. The movie was delayed time and again due to unforeseen events, one being the Covid-19 pandemic.
