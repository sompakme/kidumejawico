    Since 2017, Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott have taken the world by surprise with their love story. In April 2017 they were spotted together at Coachella, and within less than one month, Travis Scott was reportedly telling his friends that they were a couple.

    During their relationship, Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott welcomed two children, Stormi and Aire Webster. The couple has often been seen together, but they have also had periods of being apart, even though they don't always publicize their splits or the reasoning behind them.

    Their relationship is on/off, breaking up to focus on their individual careers and families, but also getting back together and announcing a pregnancy. It isn't a secret they didn't live together for most of their relationship and often showed having different interests.

    Also, fans noticed a pattern; the 'one baby daddy' in the Kardashian-Jenner family. For these reasons, as well as Kylie Jenner's dream of being a young mom, many people think Kylie Jenner might have an ulterior motive for staying with Travis Scott, and isn't necessarily about love.

    Kylie Jenner And Travis Scott Reportedly Split Shortly After They Had Their Second Child

    When Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott announced that their son has been born, most fans thought that their on/off relationship can change to something more serious. That wasn't the case.

    Within months since Aire Webster came into the world, Kylie Jenner reportedly dumped Travis Scott, shocking, once again, everyone with this decision.

    Looking back on their relationship, this wasn't the first time that they took a break. Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott broke up in October 2019 because they needed to focus on their individual careers and families.

    Shortly after, they got back together and welcomed a second child. Rumors of a wedding were emerging, but they quickly vanished when Kylie Jenner dumped Travis Scott months after Aire Webster was born.

    Fans Think Kylie Stayed With Travis For One Reason

    Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott's relationship was always unconventional since they didn't live together much, and focused on separate aspects of their lives. While some people appreciate their modern lifestyle, others don't understand why Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott get back together and have children, considering their interests seem different.

    From being great at co-parenting, but not being consistently together, Kylie Jenner's relationship with her baby daddy has a similar pattern to the one of Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick.

    Also, they think another reason might be that Kylie Jenner went to a psychic who told her she'll have two children, and Travis Scott was already the father of her first child. So, it made sense to have a second child with him.
