    American TV personality and businessman Brett Raymer has an estimated net worth of $5 million in 2012.

    Originating from Brooklyn, New York City, Brett Raymer is a graduate of Abraham Lincoln High School. When his family moved to Las Vegas, they opened a tank-building shop which they called Acrylic Tank Manufacturing (ATM). Eventually, ATM became one of the world's successful custom aquarium companies.

    Today, life at ATM is featured on the TV series called Tanked. The series features the lives of Brett Raymer and other members of his family which include his father, Irwin Raymer, sister Heather and brother-in-law Wayde King, as they work hand in hand in running the business and attending to the needs of their customers. Known for his superb confidence and his natural ability to bring in customers, Brett Raymer currently serves as the company's CEO. The show started airing in August 2011 and it has recently concluded its second season.
