    Tommy Hilfiger is an American fashion designer born in New York on March 24, 1951. As a teenager, Tommy became interested in fashion and started making his own unique outfits. He later moved to New York City and started his fashion career in the retail industry, working for various stores and companies around the city. Later, in 1985, Tommy established his own clothing company, which immediately became well-known for its unique and distinctive style.

    Tommy has some of the most costly residences in the world. Tommy Hilfiger works with designers and artists to create unique designs that give a personal touch. Overall, Tommy Hilfiger Homes offer a variety of high-quality, traditional, and classy home decor. From his stunning mansion Hollywood Hills, to a penthouse in New York, let's look at Tommy Hilfiger's multi-million dollar homes.

    Golden Beach Mansion - $17.3 million

    Tommy Hilfiger's Florida home is a luxurious Mediterranean-style villa in the prestigious Golden Beach community. The 14,000-square-foot property features six bedrooms, a pool, and a private beach. The home's interior design is inspired by the seaside location, with light and airy rooms decorated in a neutral color palette.Tommy and his wife Dee Ocleppo sold the property in 2017 for around $28 million.

    Palm Beach, Florida -$36.5million

    Tommy Hilfiger's palm Beach, Villa Florida is a stunning Mediterranean-style mansion in Miami Beach's heart.The property features three bedrooms and four bathrooms and a beautiful lakeside pool. The interior design of the residence is inspired by Mediterranean-style decor art architecture, with vibrant colors and geometric designs.Tommy paid $36.5 million for it and then listed it for $47.95 million a week later.

    Hollywood Hills Villa, California - $24.5 million.

    This Hollywood Hills villa is a stunning modernist property in the exclusive neighborhood of Los Angeles. The four-bedroom home features a pool, outdoor living spaces, and breathtaking city views. The villa's interior design is sleek and minimalist, with clean lines and a neutral color palette.

    Nantucket Beach House, Massachusetts - $27 Million

    Round Hill Estate,Manor -$31 million

    The estate is located north of New York and sits on 10- hectares. The villa is surrounded by a community with charming taste. The estate is stacked with art and antiques. The outdoors is so impressive, with a well-manicured garden and a waterfall. The estate 's interior features a fusion of Gothic and Tudor design elements, such as soaring ceilings, expansive windows, and ornate woodwork. The estate is surrounded by beautiful scenery and provides the owner a peaceful haven.

    Greenwich, Connecticut Estate-$31.4 million

    Tommy Hilfiger's primary residence is a stunning Georgian-style mansion on a 22-acre property in Greenwich, Connecticut. The house boasts eight bedrooms, a tennis court, a pool, and a private lake. The home's interior design incorporates classic and modern features, with lavish decorations and artwork gracing the walls.

    Plaza Hotel Penthouse, New York - $50 Million

    The apartment is at the renowned Plaza Hotel and has breathtaking views of Central Park. The apartment is approximately 3,000 square feet and has three bedrooms, three bathrooms, and a vast living room. The apartment's interior design is classic and sumptuous, with high ceilings, marble flooring, and fine furnishings. Since its completion in 1907, the building has been a popular location for celebrities and high-profile individuals due to its rich history.

    Island Villa, Caribbean - $200 million

    Tommy owns a villa on the exclusive island of Mustique in the Caribbean. Mustique is a small private island that is part of St. Vincent and the Grenadines and is known for its secluded beaches and exclusive villas. The Tommy Hilfiger Island Villa is a stunning property with five bedrooms and can accommodate up to ten guests. It is worth noting that Mustique is a highly exclusive and sought-after destination for luxury travelers and property investors, and many of the private villas on the island are valued at several million dollars.The island is home to the most influential celebrities.He purchased the villa in 1990, and it is now estimated to be valued approximately $200 million due to the high demand for luxury houses in the neighborhood.
