    RJ Cyler Actor

    RJ has always been an entertainer in some way or form. Just at the age of 12, he formed a dance crew with his two older brothers. Prior to moving to Los Angeles in 2013, he heard a commercial for open auditions in the Jacksonville area. Cyler got his breakthrough role as Earl in the beloved 2015 comedy-drama film ‘Me and Earl and the Dying Girl’.

    He portrayed alongside the likes of Thomas Man, Olivia Cooke, and Connie Britton. Before that, he starred in the short film Second Chances in 2013. Furthermore, he has also appeared in a couple of episodes in HBO’s Vice Principals. Moreover, Cyler has also been among the cast in one of Jim Carrey‘s produced TV show ‘ I’m Dying Up Here’ which ran from 2017 to 2018.

    RJ Cyler White Boy Rick

    Cyler starred alongside the lead characters Matthew McConaughey and Richie Merritt in the 2018 crime drama film ‘White Boy Rick’. He played as Rudell “Boo” Curry.  ‘White Boy Rick’ is the story of teenager Richard Wershe Jr., who became an undercover informant for the FBI during the 1980s and was ultimately arrested for drug-trafficking and sentenced to life in prison.

    RJ Cyler Scream

    Cyler stars as in the lead role of Deion Elliot in the third season of the VH1 slasher television series ‘Scream’. The show was issued on July 8, 2019. Also, he has portrayed Dan in the Netflix comedy-drama film, Sierra Burgess Is a Loser.

    RJ Cyler Black Lightning

    He has also shared the screen with Cress Williams, China Anne McClain, and Nafessa Williams in the second season of the CW hit series ‘Black Lightning’. In the show, RJ plays a recurring role as Todd Green.

    RJ Cyler Power Rangers

    Cyler has additionally portrayed Billy Cranston / Blue Ranger in the 2017 superhero movie based on the franchise of the same name ‘Power Rangers’. He starred opposite Naomi Scott, Elizabeth Banks, Bryan Cranston, and Dacre Montgomery. Likewise, he acted as Technical Sergeant Andy Moon alongside the likes of Brad Pitt, Ben Kingsley, Will Poulter, and Anthony Michael Hall in the 2017 war film War Machine based on the nonfiction book The Operators: The Wild and Terrifying Inside Story of America’s War.

    RJ Cyler Swamp Thing

    At the begging of 2019, Cyler joined the cast of DC Universe horror series, ‘Swamp Thing’, playing the recurring role of Jones. What’s more, he played as Adam Proteau from 2017 to 2018 in the comedy-drama TV show ‘I’m Dying Up Here’. More so, he has starred alongside Amandla Stenberg and Nick Robinson in the successful romantic film ‘Everything, Everything’.
