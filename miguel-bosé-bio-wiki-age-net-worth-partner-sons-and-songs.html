    Miguel Bosé Career

    Miguel began his acting career in 1971 after being influenced by his famous family. By 1975, he had explored different talents such as music. He recorded his first singles with the help of the Spanish producer and musician. He became a major teen idol in Spain, Italy, and all over Latin America with his hits earning him the spot in every televised song festival held in the aforementioned countries.

    In March 2007, he released his first album Papito which contained remakes of his previous songs with other major Spanish musicians. After the success of his first album, he released his second album called Papitwo which contained additional remakes of well-known songs from his career, again featuring artists such as Bimba Bosé on Alejandro Sanz on “Te Comería El Corazón” “Shoot Me in the Back” and Juanes on “Partisano”.
