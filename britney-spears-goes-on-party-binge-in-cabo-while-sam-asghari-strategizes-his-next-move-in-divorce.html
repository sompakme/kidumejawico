    Britney Spears is continuing to let loose while she navigates her ongoing divorce from Sam Asghari. Most recently, the pop star was seen partying in Cabo, where she had an embarrassing wardrobe malfunction caught on video.

    In a video acquired by Page Six, the singer was captured dancing at Baja Diablo in Cabo, Mexico on Sunday. However, while she was dancing, one of the sleeves of her red dress slipped and came close to revealing her breast. Despite the mishap, Spears appeared unfazed as she carried on dancing. A short while later, she casually adjusted her dress to ensure she remained covered.

    For the occasion, Britney accessorized her attire with white-rimmed sunglasses and her customary choker necklace, styling her hair in a tousled bun. She was seen spending time with a female companion as well as her bodyguard.

    Britney Admits She’s “Embarrassed” By Her Recent Partying

    After footage from Britney’s near-wadrobe malfunction made its way online, the former Disney star addressed the incident on Instagram, admitting to feeling embarrassed by the footage that’s since gone virla.

    “I’m embarrassed as hell !!! Like a lost idiot I heard music and horns from my car,” she captioned a screenshot of her in the infamous red dress. “I walked in like a deer in headlights and had no idea I was being recorded from all sides !!! I’m only human and I have no idea what the hell is going on with my hair but I made a pit stop in Mexico.”

    Britney also revealed her next vacation plans, saying, “I’m headed to Italy […] now to grab my favorite spaghetti and meat BALLS!!! I know the owner there so hopefully he can get me in through the back door.”

    Sam Is Focusing On His Career, While Britney Keeps Partying

    Britney Spears has been making headlines for her wild antics since Sam Asghari filed for divorce on August 16th. But while she’s keeping herself in the public eye – including throwing a risqué divorce party – Sam has been maintaining a low profile, as reports say he’s focusing on his career while going through the divorce proceedings.

    At the end of last month, TMZ reported that inside sources disclosed Sam's plans to secure fresh acting opportunities once the SAG-AFTRA strikes conclude. Additionally, it seems that Sam is exploring potential hosting roles in addition to acting gigs.

    Furthermore, those in the know suggest that Sam is dedicating significant time to his family, particularly his mother, who was recently discharged from the hospital following an accident-related injury.

    Speaking to Us Weekly, sources say Sam isn’t bothered by Britney’s recent partying. Rather, it’s made him confident in his decision to leave their marriage after only 14 months.

    “Sam isn’t sure if Britney was trying to get his attention on social media by throwing a divorce party and partying with other men, but it’s none of his business,” an insider revealed. “It only reinforces his feelings on the split and knowing he did the right thing.”

    Sam hasn’t commented on Britney’s recent wardrobe slip-up in Cabo.
