    Dean McDermott Is Demanding To Get Spousal Support And Joint Custody From Tori Spelling

    By the time 2024 began, it seemed clear to everyone that Tori Spelling and Dean McDermott had gone their separate ways. However, the former couple had hit many rough patches before, only to reconcile and recommit themselves to each other.

    As a result, it took some observers by surprise when the world learned that Tori Spelling filed for divorce in March 2024. That said, once fans learned what Spelling had done, predicting what would happen next seemed easy. After all, given Spelling and McDermott's dramatic history, nobody should have expected them to have a quick and drama-free divorce.

    In May 2024, an article from The Daily Mail revealed that McDermott's legal team responded to Spelling. Based on what that report revealed, it seemed extremely likely that Spelling and McDermott would have a bitter divorce. After all, McDermott's team told the court that he was demanding spousal support from Spelling and joint custody of their kids.

    According to an article from Today, McDermott and Spelling have five children together. The former couple's children are Liam, Stella, Hattie, Finn, and Beau McDermott. Based on Dean McDermott's filing, he wants to share legal and physical custody of their kids with Spelling.

    However, McDermott and Spelling seem destined to have another major bone of contention in their divorce. The reason for that is Spelling and McDermott are both seeking money from each other as part of their divorce.
