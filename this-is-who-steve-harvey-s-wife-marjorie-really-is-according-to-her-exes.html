    Who Is Marjorie Harvey?

    Marjorie Harvey is from the United States. She grew up in Memphis, Tennessee, and attended the University of Memphis even though she later dropped out of school in order to pursue a career in the fashion industry for which she has gained a reputation as a style icon.

    Harvey is also a philanthropist, she and her husband are owners of a charity group called Steve & Marjorie Harvey foundation whose focus is to support underprivileged children and teenagers.

    Marjorie Harvey has been married three times. Her first marriage was to Jim Townsend, with whom she had two children. Her second marriage was to Darnell Woods, with whom she had one child. Her third and current husband is Steve Harvey, whom she married in 2007.

    Harvey is known for her lavish lifestyle and expensive taste. She often shares her fashion tips and style choices on social media, where she has a significant following of over 2 million.

    She also frequently appears on her husband's television show, "The Steve Harvey Show," and has been a guest on other talk shows as well. She is a dedicated mother not only to her own children but also to her stepchildren. She is well-known for her strong family values and is vocal about the importance family has in her life.

    Not only is she a successful fashion designer and philanthropist, but she is also an example of a loving and devoted parent.

    Marjorie Harvey's First Husband Was Jim Townsend

    Marjorie Harvey's first husband, Jim Townsend, made headlines in recent years after reportedly writing a tell-all memoir about his marriage to Marjorie Harvey in the 1990s.

    According to Radar Online, Jim decided to speak out about his relationship with Harvey, claiming that he has "no allegiance" to her. "I wrote two-thirds of the book in prison,” he claimed. "she showed her hand, I’m going … to tell my story. It doesn’t matter if she knows about it.” He allegedly did not want to take any rash actions because of his children.

    Harvey and Townsend were married in the 1990s and had two children together. However, their marriage was allegedly plagued by Jim's involvement in illegal activities which ultimately led to his arrest and imprisonment.

    According to Radar Online, Marjorie was reportedly under investigation by the FBI during their marriage, with investigators labeling Townsend as a 'drug lord'. Although Marjorie was never charged with a drug crime, her association with Townsend brought unwanted attention to her.

    Jim Townsend was eventually caught in an undercover sting operation and was sentenced to life imprisonment in 1992 for "conspiracy to possess with the intent to distribute" an illegal substance. However, in January 2017, former President Barack Obama pardoned Jim.

    Darnell Woods Was Marjorie Harvey's Second Husband

    Marjorie Harvey's second marriage was to Darnell Woods, whom she wed in the early 2000s. Little is known about their relationship, except that Woods like Townsend was a substance dealer and the marriage was relatively short-lived.

    Harvey and Woods had one child together Lori now known as Lori Harvey, who dated and recently broke up with Michael B. Jordan. The exact dates of Harvey and Woods' marriage and divorce are not widely reported.

    Despite the lack of information about their marriage, Darnell Woods has remained largely silent about his relationship with her. He has not spoken publicly about their marriage, and there are no reports of him making any public statements about his ex-wife, Marjorie Harvey. She has also not spoken about her marriage to Woods publicly.

    All About Marjorie Harvey And Steve Harvey's Love Story

    Marjorie Harvey and Steve Harvey's love story is one for the books. They first met in 1990 at a comedy club, where Steve was performing. However, the timing wasn't right because Steve Harvey was faced with so many difficulties at the time, which led to their calling off the relationship. In 2005 the duo reconnected through mutual friends and dated for about a year, Steve Harvey proposed, and in June 2007, they got married.

    Their love story is one of true friendship, as they consider each other best friends. "It's the first time either of us has had someone we can talk to about everything," Marjorie told People.

    Their marriage also brought together their children from past marriages as one big family, which includes a total of seven children. Steve Harvey credited Marjorie for changing the way he exists and for showing him what a healthy adult relationship looks like.

    Steve Harvey, unlike Marjorie Harvey's ex-husband, Jim Townsend has been very vocal and expressive of his love and admiration for her.
