Bethany Meilani Hamilton (born February 8, 1990) is an American professional surfer who survived a shark attack and had her left arm amputated. Her book Soul Surfer: A True Story of Faith, Family, and Fighting to Get Back on the Board, published in 2004, chronicled her journey back to the water. The feature film Soul Surfer, which was based on the book and additional interviews, was released in April 2011. Since losing her arm, she has featured on a slew of other television programmes.
