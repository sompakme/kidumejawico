    The Red Mango Cafe & Juice Bar franchise offers a vibrant opportunity for aspiring entrepreneurs in the food industry. With a menu that includes frozen yogurt, smoothies, juices, wraps, and more, Red Mango has become a popular destination for health-conscious consumers.

    The initial investment to open a Red Mango franchise ranges from $239,000 to $591,000. This includes the franchise fee, along with other startup expenses such as real estate, equipment, supplies, and working capital.

    In order to qualify, potential franchisees must have a minimum net worth of $350,000 and at least $200,000 in liquid capital. Veterans can enjoy a 50% discount on the traditional-store franchise fee.

    The ongoing royalty fee is 6% of gross sales, while the ad royalty fee is 3%. The franchise term lasts for 5-10 years, with the option to renew. Red Mango provides training programs, ongoing support, and various marketing initiatives to help franchisees succeed.

    Red Mango Franchise Overview

    Red Mango is a well-established franchise in the frozen yogurt and juice bar industry. Founded in 2007, the brand has grown to include 58 locations as of 2023. Red Mango offers a diverse menu that includes all-natural frozen yogurts, fruit smoothies, cold-squeezed juices, salads, wraps, and more.

    The franchise is part of Brix Holdings LLC, a parent company that provides support and resources to its franchisees. The CEO of Red Mango is Sherif Mityas, and the corporate address is located in Dallas, Texas. The company has a strong online presence, with active social media accounts on Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn.

    Red Mango has been franchising since 2007 and offers new franchise opportunities throughout the United States and worldwide.

    Red Mango Franchise Requirements

    To become a Red Mango franchisee and join the vibrant world of healthy and delicious food, there are certain requirements you need to meet. These requirements ensure that you have the necessary qualifications and mindset to succeed as a Red Mango franchise owner.

  • Minimum Net Worth: You should have a minimum net worth of $350,000.
  • Liquid Capital: You must have at least $200,000 in liquid capital.
  • Shared Values and Goals: It is crucial that you share the values and goals of the Red Mango brand. This includes a commitment to providing high-quality, healthy food options and exceptional customer service.
  • No Absentee Ownership: Red Mango does not allow absentee ownership, meaning you should be actively involved in the day-to-day operations of your franchise.
  • Dedicated Team: Running a Red Mango location requires a dedicated team. You will need approximately 10-15 employees to support the smooth functioning of your franchise.
  • No Home-Based or Part-Time Business: Red Mango franchises cannot be run from a home or mobile unit, and part-time ownership is also not permitted.
  • Exclusive Territory: As a Red Mango franchisee, you have the opportunity to secure an exclusive territory, giving you the right to operate in a fixed area without competition from other Red Mango units.

    • By meeting these requirements, you will be on your way to embarking on a rewarding journey as a Red Mango franchise owner. With these guidelines in place, Red Mango ensures that its franchisees are well-equipped to deliver the highest quality products and service that the brand is known for.
