    What is the ClickFunnels Affiliate Program?

    The ClickFunnels Affiliate Program is a marketing initiative that allows individuals to earn passive income by promoting ClickFunnels products and services. Through affiliate marketing, participants can leverage their online presence and networks to generate revenue through commissions.

    Affiliate marketing involves promoting products or services on behalf of a company and earning a commission for each sale made through your unique affiliate ID. In the case of ClickFunnels, affiliates earn a recurring payout of 30% for all active accounts signed up using their affiliate ID.

    Recurring Payout

    A significant advantage of the ClickFunnels Affiliate Program is the recurring payout structure. When someone signs up for ClickFunnels through your affiliate link, you receive a recurring commission every month as long as they remain an active customer. This creates a steady stream of passive income that can continue for the duration of the customer’s subscription.

    Additionally, affiliates can earn up to $89.10 per month for each trial signup from the ClickFunnels Challenge. This incentivizes affiliates to drive new customers to try out ClickFunnels, increasing their earning potential.

    ClickFunnels Sticky Cookies

    ClickFunnels utilizes sticky cookies as an integral part of their affiliate program. These special cookies are designed to track the referrals made by affiliates, ensuring that they receive commissions for sales generated by their referrals. What makes these sticky cookies unique is that they continue to track referrals even if the referrals don’t use the affiliate link for future purchases. This feature is particularly beneficial for affiliates as it allows them to earn recurring commissions from their referrals, maximizing their earning potential.

    Tracking referrals is crucial for both ClickFunnels and their affiliates. It ensures that affiliates are properly credited for the customers they bring in, even if those customers navigate directly to the website or use a different link during subsequent visits. With sticky cookies, affiliates can confidently promote ClickFunnels knowing that their efforts will be rewarded with recurring commissions.

    By implementing sticky cookies, ClickFunnels has created a fair and transparent system that acknowledges the valuable contributions of their affiliates. This feature not only motivates affiliates to continue promoting ClickFunnels’ products and services but also establishes a long-lasting partnership between the brand and its affiliates. The ClickFunnels sticky cookies provide a seamless tracking mechanism, giving affiliates the confidence to build profitable businesses by leveraging the recurring commissions earned through their referrals.
