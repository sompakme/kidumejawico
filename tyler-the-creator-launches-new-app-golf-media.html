    Tyler The Creator Launches New App 'Golf Media'

    07 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Have you ever wanted to pick Tyler The Creator‘s brain? Or dare we say it, reside in it even if just for a moment? Well, now you can.

    The Odd Future front man announced his new app titled Golf Media and described it as “basically my brain, in one place.”

    The app, which will be available for download Wednesday (April 8) at 9PM PST will be on Android and iPhone. To purchase for free, users can register through golfwang.com and receive two months of free programming. FADER reports users will also receive the first issue of Golf Magazine, which is Tyler’s forthcoming print publication. Those who don’t register through the site will pay $4.99.

    Tyler’s manager Christian Clancy said users can opt out of the service at any point and described it as “A bunch of different things that are original, curated and constantly changing. Original series, content, live streaming, radio, tour stuff, golf wang, interactive and whatever other buzzwords that sound mildly annoying and marketing-like.”

    Great, so who wants to go and chill in Tyler’s head for a bit?
