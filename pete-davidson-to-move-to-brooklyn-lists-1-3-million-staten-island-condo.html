    Pete Davidson Is making good on his words. After revealing in February he wants to move to Brooklyn, the comedian has put his Staten Island home on the market for $1.3 million.

    The condo is located in one of the island’s charming neighborhoods, Georgetown. It offers over 1,500 square feet of living space, including 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. Its other features include marble waterfall counters, a spacious balcony, and an open concept layout. The home also offers a clear view of downtown New York’s skyline.

    Jason Mitchell Group’s Amy Arnone and Marissa Polo are representing the listing. Speaking to PEOPLE, Amy expressed excitement over helping Pete with his move.

    "We are elated to be a part of the network of professionals Pete surrounds himself with," she shared. "Pete's property is well appointed with a modern interior, luxurious amenities and spectacular views."

    Before moving into the Statent Island condo, Pete revealed he was still living with his mother after they bought a $1.3 million home on the island together.

    “So, I live with my mom… well we bought a house together, but nobody believes that. So, I live with my mom, kinda,” Pete said during an appearance on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon.

    He continued, “So I have, like, a basement that’s mine, but that’s, like, an apartment, so I live underneath her.”

    It was first reported in April 2021 that Pete bought himself the bachelor pad, which Kim Kardashian was spotted visiting during their brief relationship that started later in the year.

    There’s no word on where in Brooklyn Pete is hoping to relocate to, or if he’s already found a place. But there’s speculation that his new girlfriend Emily Ratajkowski may influence where he ends up.

    Reports say the pair have been dating for a few months, though news of their romance only broke in November. Emily just finalized a divorce from her husband of four years, Sebastian Bear-McClard, after rumors of infidelity. Pete and Sebastian were actually friends before he began dating Emily.

    Emily has her own apartment in New York’s downtown along with her 1-year-old son. While there’s no evidence to suggest Pete is moving specifically to be close to her, he may keep Emily’s location in mind when touring apartments.

    Pete’s condo remains still on the apartment and a million-dollar price tag isn’t bad considering New York’s typical prices.
