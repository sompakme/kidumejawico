    The Klov Forum, a popular online hub for arcade gaming enthusiasts, recently fell victim to a hacking incident that has compromised user accounts. Scammers managed to gain unauthorized access to the forum, exploiting vulnerabilities in the system’s security measures. As a result, they created fake for-sale threads using hijacked identities, deceiving unsuspecting members of the community.

    The administrators of the Klov Forum are taking this breach seriously and have taken the site offline for thorough investigation and restoration. They are working tirelessly to identify the extent of the hack and ensure that all necessary measures are in place to prevent future incidents.

    Notable Projects Associated with Klov Forum

    Alongside the Klov Forum, several notable projects are associated with the community. These initiatives contribute to the preservation and appreciation of arcade gaming history and provide valuable resources for enthusiasts.

    The Killer List of Videogames (KLOV)

    The Killer List of Videogames (KLOV) is a comprehensive game database that catalogs coin-operated video games. Showcasing thousands of titles, KLOV provides detailed information about each game, including release dates, developer and publisher information, gameplay descriptions, and cabinet images. It serves as a go-to resource for collectors, researchers, and anyone interested in exploring the vast world of arcade gaming. The KLOV database on the Klov Forum is constantly updated by knowledgeable community members, ensuring its accuracy and relevance.

    The Arcade Preservation Society

    The Arcade Preservation Society, a dedicated group of Klov Forum members, focuses on the census and preservation of rare arcade machines. By documenting and sharing information about these vintage cabinets, the society plays a crucial role in safeguarding arcade gaming history for future generations. Through their efforts, rare and unique arcade games are not only preserved but also celebrated within the community. The Arcade Preservation Society’s work underscores the collective commitment of Klov Forum members to the long-term preservation of arcade gaming culture.
