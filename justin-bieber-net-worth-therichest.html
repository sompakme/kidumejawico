    Justin Bieber is a Canadian singing sensation who has an estimated net worth of $305 million. In addition to being a pop singer, Bieber is also an actor and songwriter. Forbes considered him as the world’s ninth most powerful celebrity in 2013. He was third in the ranks in 2011 and 2012.

    Scooter Braun discovered Justin Bieber and later became Bieber’s manager. He saw him through his videos on YouTube. Subsequently, the Raymond Braun Media Group signed him. Afterward, a record executive, L.A. Reid, offered Beiber a recording contract.

    In November 2009, they released his first extended play with the title My World. It earned a U.S. platinum record. Afterward, he had his first studio album My World 2.0. It won a platinum record in the USA and hit the peak charts in other countries.

    Last November 2011 was his second studio album, Under the Mistletoe. It immediately debuted at #1 on Billboard 200 upon its release. In June 2012, he released his third album, "Believe." Similar to his recent album, it debuted at #1 on Billboard 200. Last 2012, Bieber was able to sell a total of 15 albums worldwide.

    Bieber became the Artist of the Year in the American Music Awards. At the 53rd Grammy Awards, he received a nomination for Best Pop Vocal Album and Best New Artist Awards.
