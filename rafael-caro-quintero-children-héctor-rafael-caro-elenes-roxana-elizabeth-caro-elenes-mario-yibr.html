    Rafael Caro Quintero, a Mexican drug trafficker cited as a pioneer of the drug trade in Mexico and has been described as one of the pre-eminent drug traffickers of his generation had four children with his wife María Elizabeth Elenes Lerma.

    The eldest son of the “Narco de narcos” and Mara Elizabeth Elenes is Héctor Rafael Caro Elenes.  He was born on December 18, 1975. He is 47.

    Héctor Rafael is a member of the Guadalajara Jockey Club, where he competes in Jalisco Equestrian Association events.

    He won the gold medal in team jumping at the Central American and Caribbean Games in Colombia in 2006.

    Rafael Caro Quintero’s second daughter with Mara Elizabeth Elenes Lerma is Roxana Elizabeth Caro Elenes.

    Roxana was born on the 17th of January, 1978. She is 45. Roxana is the wife of Jes Briseo Gómez Espaa.

    Mario Tibrán Caro Elenes was born on June 11, 1983. He is 39 years old. Mario and his brothers are named on the US Treasury Department’s list.

    Henoch Emilio Caro Elenes was born on March 15, 1980. He is 42 years old. Henoch owns number 5 Plaza Real del Valle in the San José del Valle subdivision of Tlajomulco de Ziga.
