    Weddings are pretty and all, but what about all the parties that come right before? Before tying the knot, future grooms and brides-to-be celebrate the final days of single life with bachelor and bachelorette parties. Many bachelors get their best friends together and take a trip to the local bar to celebrate the end of singledom.

    Related: 10 Celebrities Who Married A Non-Famous Person

    However, for some celebrities, your average bachelor parties just won't cut it. From raucous multi-day Las Vegas trips to dinner that costs of $3,000, to low-key, star-studded hangouts, these celebs have some pretty enviable bachelor parties. Scroll down to check out the 10 most awesome celebrity bachelor parties we wish we could have attended.

    Joe Jonas

    Popstar Joe Jonas tied the knot with Game of Thrones actress Sophie Turner in not one, but two ceremonies. The couple married first in Las Vegas on May 1st, 2019, then had a second late-June nuptials in the south of France.

    Joe Jonas flaunted his awesome bachelor party on Instagram. "THE BOY. THE BOYS. THE BOYS. Incredible weekend full of celebration In Ibiza. Love you all," he captioned a series of pics of his crew downing shots, smoking cigars, and playing a very legit looking soccer match on a full field. Looks like fun!

    Justin Bieber

    In 2019, "Sorry" singer Justin Bieber said "I Do" to supermodel Hailey Baldwin. The tween-sensation may be known for his wild-child ways; however, apparently his bachelor party was far from crazy. While Hailey Baldwin hit the club with model pals like Kendall Jenner, Bieber's night was less wild.

    Related: 10 Ridiculously Expensive Things That Justin Bieber Bought

    Bieber asked rapper Post Malone to plan his bachelor party, and Posty reported that a very "chill" time would be had by all. "We're going to have a nice, chill time," he said. "A nice, wholesome time." Possible activities included hanging out and fishing. With both Posty and the Biebs there, we wish we scored an invite.

    George Clooney

    Hollywood superstar George Clooney married human-rights lawyer Amal Alamuddin in a lavish Venice ceremony. The night before, Clooney took a group of his best pals to one of his favorite Italian eateries, Ristorante da Ivo. The group ate their way through five carb-filled courses and two desserts.

    Despite earning $27,283 an hour, Clooney didn't pay a cent for his $3,336 meal. The owner waved the fee for his long-time friend, and, instead, Clooney signed a wine bottle with a sweet note, thanking Ristorante da Ivo for a perfect night.

    Miles Teller

    32-year-old Whiplash star Miles Teller tied the knot with model Keleigh Sperry in September 2019. Before he got to the altar, the actor had a lavish Las Vegas bachelor party at the Palms Casino Resort in Las Vegas.

    Nick Jonas

    The second JoBro to appear on this list, Nick Jonas, said "I do," to actress Priyanka Chopra, and the pair threw some fun-filled bachelor and bachelorette parties.

    Related: 10 Times Nick Jonas & Priyanka Chopra Were Relationship Goals

    Nick Jonas's star-studded event featured lots of fun, lots of sun, and some pretty heavy product placement. He posed with a bottle of Elit vodka on a yacht for some Instagram pics with the caption "Bachelor party weekend is officially underway people." Puzzlingly, this list of endorsements included the scooter brand Lime. "I was searching for a fun way for my groomsmen to be mobile and ready to roll," the Jonas brother captioned a shot of him with a fleet of Lime scooters.

    Kanye West

    Kanye kept it low key before marrying reality TV star Kim Kardashian. Kim hit the Louvre, the Eiffel Tower, and a star-studded dinner in a heavily embellished Balmain dress, and hubby-to-be Kanye celebrated his final days of singledom both in Paris and in LA.

    Apparently, his bachelor party was more of a chill hangout than an all-night rager. But, with famous friends like Tyga and Jay-Z, we're sure the night was one to remember!

    John Legend

    John Legend also opted for a more low-key night. The R&B legend enjoyed a dinner and night a night of clubbing in The Hamptons. Well, maybe his night wasn't totally chill.

    Justin Timberlake

    Before tying the knot with actress Jessica Biel, Justin Timberlake took 25 lucky friends on an international escapade. The singer took his pals to Las Vegas and crossed the border to Mexico.

    Brad Pitt

    In 2012, Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie, two of the most recognizable superstars in Hollywood, tied the knot to form a supernova team. Before Brad said, "I do," he enjoyed a night of boozing and smoking in Cannes.
