    23 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Though the clock continues to tock for John Legend‘s forthcoming effort Love In The Future, the smooth maestro releases the cover art and track list to pass the time. With Rick Ross already making a cameo on the lead single “Who Do We Think We Are,” the set also features Legend’s Homeschool label signee Stacy Barthe and Seal on the deluxe version. Love In The Future hits stores Sept. 3.

    “Love In The Future”- Standard Version:
    1. Love In The Future (Intro)
    2. The Beginning…
    3. Open Your Eyes
    4. Made To Love
    5. Who Do We Think We Are (Ft. Rick Ross)
    6. All Of Me
    7. Hold On Longer
    8. Save The Night
    9. Tomorrow
    10. What If I Told You? (Interlude)
    11. Dreams
    12. Wanna Be Loved
    13. Angel (Interlude) (Ft. Stacy Barthe)
    14. You & I (Nobody In The World)
    15. Asylum
    16. Caught Up

    “Love In The Future” – Deluxe Version:
    1. Love In The Future (Intro)
    2. The Beginning…
    3. Open Your Eyes
    4. Made To Love
    5. Who Do We Think We Are (Ft. Rick Ross)
    6. All Of Me
    7. Hold On Longer
    8. Save The Night
    9. Tomorrow
    10. What If I Told You? (Interlude)
    11. Dreams
    12. Wanna Be Loved
    13. Angel (Interlude) (Ft. Stacy Barthe)
    14. You & I (Nobody In The World)
    15. Asylum
    16. Caught Up
    Bonus Tracks:
    17. So Gone
    18. We Loved It (Ft. Seal)
    19. Aim High
    20. For The First Time
